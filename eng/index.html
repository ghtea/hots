I hope this site helps you to understand HotS deeper. Use this site as a complement to other sites like official one. I got helped from HotsApi in collecting data.

For newcomers (show/hide)
  • This site is made bymbcat who collected HotS game score data, analyzed and made visualization/application (the first upload：2019. 6.)
  • Check other sites like official one for abilities of heroes
  • Read pages in order of Role → Hero、upper page → lower page
  • Cho, Gall, The Lost Viking were excluded because of data collecting problems
  • The contents in this site are based on result of the data analysis which means the influence of analysist's opinion is small
